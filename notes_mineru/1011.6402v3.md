## 3）主题笔记（Topic Notes）
### OFI的定义与构造
依据E1：OFI is the sum of order book event contributions（e_n）over a time interval，capturing net supply/demand imbalance at best bid/ask levels. The e_n variable measures changes in bid/ask queue sizes or prices.

### 标签定义与预测目标
依据E2、E3：The prediction target is mid-price changes（ΔP_k）in ticks over10-second intervals. The label is computed as（P_k - P_{k-1}）/δ where δ=1 cent.

### OFI vs Trade Imbalance的预测能力对比
依据E4、E6：OFI has higher explanatory power for mid-price changes than trade imbalance（65% vs32% R²）. Trade imbalance becomes statistically insignificant when used with OFI in regression.

### Price Impact与市场深度的关系
依据E5：The price impact coefficient（β）is inversely proportional to market depth（AD_i），supported by log-linear regression with average R² of74%.

### 模型与评价
依据E2、E3、E6：The model uses OLS regression on half-hour subsamples. Evaluation metrics include R²（65% average for OFI）and coefficient significance（β significant in97% of subsamples）.
