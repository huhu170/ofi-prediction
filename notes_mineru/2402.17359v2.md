## 3）主题笔记（Topic Notes）
### LOB 基本定义与关键特征
依据证据 E1, E6：The LOB consists of discrete tick-separated price levels（E1）. Key features include mid-price（average of best bid/ask）、spread（bid-ask difference）、queue size，and market impact（price movement from trading）（E6）.

### 点过程模型的对比
依据证据 E2, E3, E5：Poisson models assume independent order arrivals but fail to capture volatility clustering（E3）. Hawkes Processes use endogenous excitation to better replicate volatility clustering、Epps effect，and inter-order arrival time tails（E2, E3, E5）.

### 深度学习模型的优势与局限
依据证据 E4, E7, E9：Conditional Wasserstein GANs model realistic LOB state transitions（E4）but are black-box（E7）. GAN models show clear market impact trends，unlike Poisson/Hawkes（E9）.

### 市场冲击的重要性
依据证据 E6, E9：Market impact is critical for simulator realism—realistic models must capture trend lines for execution strategies（E6, E9）.
